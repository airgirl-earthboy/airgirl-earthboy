## Improvements 🚧
- Use either polygon collider (research how to edit) or capsule collider to prevent characters from getting stuck (hair should not be included in collider)
- Physics was not exactly accurate (characters moved weirdly, player can hold W/up arrow and fly through level, can move directly up instead of jumping)
- Level 1 was too easy (add obstacles, make larger playing area with a bunch of gears in trickier places)
- Make levels longer and different in their own ways (ex. one level could be used to show each of the character's abilities by forcing them to learn it, another has the player combine them)
- Lack of a down control was confusing (used to concept of 4th input, like "ducking" animation)
- Lack of moving animations made character movement awkward (make current bouncing animation the idle animation)
- Figure out a way to find need for both characters (ex. player could only get gear if the player stacks the two characters together)
- Make both characters have their respective abilities (ex. Airgirl could float instead of sink, Earthboy could have a platform summon at his own will) to force player to have to think and make more difficult choices
- Add sound effects (walking, collecting gears)
- Allowing both characters to be controlled at once was a bit complicated
- Add features (ex. green water area can only be gone through by Earthboy or Airgirl, similar to original games)
- Players should both have to reach the gate even after all gears are collected (would make more sense in alignment with original game)
